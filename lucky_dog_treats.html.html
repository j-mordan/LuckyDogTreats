Our Story

Started with one rescue.
Grown into a movement.

Lucky Dog Treats was born when... We wanted to give back, so we started baking.

Every bag sold directly funds meals, medical care, and enrichment activities for dogs in local (our partnered if you get them) shelters. We keep overhead low and give the rest.

  • Transparent finances — our full annual report is publicly available (doesn't apply if not a business)
  • No intermediaries — we work directly with local shelters
  • Small-batch baking ensures freshness and quality in every bag
  • Packaging is 100% recyclable or compostable (remove if untrue)

Where your money goes

Daily meals at local/partner shelters Nutritious kibble and fresh water for every dog in our network, every single day.

🏡
Enrichment and adoption support Toys, training sessions, and adoption event costs to help dogs find homes faster.
